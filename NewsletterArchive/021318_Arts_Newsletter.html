Kickstarter Art + Feminism Wikipedia Edit-a-thon

A 2011 Wikimedia Foundation survey found that less than 10 percent of its contributors identify as female. Female-identified artists are also greatly underrepresented in articles on the site compared to male artists.

Join Kickstarter and nine partners — including Rhizome, ARTS.BLACK, Pioneer Works, and Black Lunch Table — at our office in Brooklyn on March 4 for an all-day communal updating of Wikipedia entries on subjects related to art and feminism.
