Rolling into the Future
You may not know Shane Chen by name, but you’ve probably seen — or ridden — his most famous invention. The Hovertrax, launched on Kickstarter in 2013, started the hoverboard craze, which saw countless imitations of Chen’s original design flooding store shelves and sidewalks around the world. Now he’s back with a new electric vehicle that he hopes will further revolutionize personal transportation. Is the Solowheel Iota destined to be the next big thing? Take a look and decide for yourself.
