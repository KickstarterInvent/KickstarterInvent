Making robots accessible to everyone
Tianqi Sun wants to help you learn robotics. The Beijing-based AI researcher envisions a future where programmable robots are as flexible and ubiquitous as today’s mobile devices, adapting to take on whatever applications we can imagine. As a starting point, he’s created HEXA, a robotics platform with an intuitive development kit that makes it possible to achieve complex movements and interactions with a few lines of code. With its built-in cameras, spatial sensors, and spider-like legs, the nimble all-terrain robot can do a lot. It’s just waiting for you to teach it some new tricks.
