"To invent, you need a good imagination and a pile of junk."
-Thomas Edison

Have you ever looked at a brilliant Kickstarter campaign and gone, "Why didn’t I think of that?" Well, it’s time to put your imagination to work and turn that proverbial pile of junk into something exciting and new. We’re collaborating with the how-to experts at Instructables to bring you the Invention Challenge, a chance to showcase your brightest ideas and invent something to share with the world. To take part, create something that completes a task, solves a problem, or makes life a little more fun.

Submit your invention here for a chance to win some great creativity-enabling prizes that got their start on Kickstarter.
